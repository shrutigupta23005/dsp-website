# Delhi Shoe Palace Website — Owner's Guide

Welcome to your website's admin panel! This guide explains how to manage your products, check views, block users, and change store settings.

---

## Logging In
1. Go to: **[delhishoepalace.com/admin](https://delhishoepalace.com/admin)**
2. **Email**: `admin@delhishoepalace.com`
3. **Password**: `Admin@123`
   *(Please change this password immediately after logging in for the first time to secure your account!)*

---

## Adding a New Product
To add a new shoe to your website:
1. Click on **Products** in the left sidebar.
2. Click the **+ Add Product** button (top right).
3. Fill in the product details:
   - **Product Name**: e.g., *Nike Air Max 270* or *Bata Formal Classic*.
   - **Description**: A few lines describing the fit, comfort, or material.
   - **Price**: The price in Rupees (e.g., `4999` — do not enter symbols like ₹, just the numbers).
   - **Category**: Choose *Men*, *Women*, or *Kids*.
   - **Subcategory**: Choose the appropriate subcategory (e.g., *Sports*, *Casual*, *Formal*).
   - **Brand**: Select the brand of the shoe.
   - **Sizes**: Click the size buttons or type in custom sizes that are currently available.
4. Click **Create Product**.
5. Once the product is created, scroll down to the **Image Manager** section to add photos:
   - Drag and drop photos from your computer or phone into the upload box.
   - For your best photo (the main front-facing view), select **Primary** as the image type.
   - For additional photos (different angles, packaging, lifestyle), select **Gallery**.
6. Click **Save Images**.

---

## Editing a Product
To change the price, description, or sizes of an existing shoe:
1. Click **Products** in the left sidebar.
2. Find the product in the list, then click the **Pencil (Edit)** icon next to it.
3. Make your modifications.
4. Click **Save Changes**.

---

## Marking a Product as "New Arrival" or "Trending"
To show special badges (like "New" or "Trending") on specific products:
1. Go to edit the product.
2. Under **Status**, select one of the following:
   - **Available**: Regular product.
   - **New Arrival**: Shows a "New" badge and lists it under the New Arrivals section.
   - **Trending**: Shows a "Trending" badge and pushes it to the trending section.
   - **Limited Stock**: Highlights that there are only a few pairs left to encourage quick inquiries.
3. Click **Save**.

---

## Featuring a Product on the Home Page
To display key products in the main showcase slider on the front home page:
1. Go to edit the product.
2. Toggle the switch for **Mark as Featured Product** to **ON**.
3. Click **Save**.
   *(Tip: Only feature about 8–12 products at any given time to keep the home page looking clean and fast.)*

---

## Checking Views and Wishlists (Analytics)
To see how well your shoes are performing:
1. Click **Dashboard** in the left sidebar.
2. Look at the overview metrics:
   - **Most Viewed**: Shoes that people are clicking on and reading about the most.
   - **Most Wishlisted**: Shoes that registered users have saved to their personal wishlists.
3. Click on the **Analytics** tab in the sidebar for graphs showing views over time and brand popularity.

---

## Blocking a User
If a user is spamming the site or writing fake reviews/inquiries, you can block their access:
1. Click **Users** in the left sidebar.
2. Find the user in the list and click the **Lock** icon next to their name.
3. Confirm that you want to block the user. They will no longer be able to log in or save wishlists.

---

## Changing Store Hours, Address, or WhatsApp Number
If your store relocates or you change numbers:
1. Click **Settings** in the left sidebar.
2. Update the text fields with the new store address, WhatsApp phone number (make sure to start with country code `91` followed by your 10-digit number without any spaces, plus signs, or hyphens), or map location URL.
3. Click **Save Settings**.
